Getting Started as a Seller
1
Log in to Seller Centre
Go to the Seller Centre and log in using your approved seller account credentials.
2
Fill in Shop Information
Provide a shop name, upload a logo, and create a brief description of your business.
3
Complete Seller Verification
Submit a valid government-issued ID (driver's license, passport, or national ID) and complete the KYC process.
4
Set Up Shipping & Payout
Set your product pickup address and link a bank account for payments in the Finance section.
5
Add Products
Upload your first product with detailed photos and descriptions to start selling.
Requirements for New Sellers
  • Active mobile number and email address
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Bank account details for withdrawals
While individual sellers may start immediately, it is highly recommended to register your business with the DTI and/or BIR for long-term compliance.
