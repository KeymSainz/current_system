KEY FEATURES:

================================================================================
TECHNICAL DETAILS:
================================================================================

Database Table: owner_inventory
- Stores product snapshots at time of purchase
- Links to owner, supplier, product, payment
- Tracks quantities and pricing
- Indexed for performance

Payment Flow:
1. Owner completes PayMongo payment
2. paymongo_return.php or paymongo_webhook.php triggered
3. Payment status updated to "paid"
4. addProductsToOwnerInventory() called
5. Products inserted into owner_inventory
6. Owner sees in "My Inventory"
7. Supplier sees in "Owner Purchases"

Security:
- Session-based authentication
- Role-based access control
- Prepared statements (SQL injection protection)
- Payment verification via PayMongo API
